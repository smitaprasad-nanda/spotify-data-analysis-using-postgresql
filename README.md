# spotify-data-analysis-using-postgresql

Problem statement: Analyze spotify streaming and engagement data to uncover insights into artist performance, track popularity, and user interaction patterns, enabling data-driven decisions for content propmotion, marketing optimization, and audience engagement strategies.

Approach:

1) Explored high-impact tracks by identifying songs with more than 1 billion streams.
2) Measured audience engagement by calculating total comments and likes on licensed and official video tracks, providing insight into content authenticity and user interaction.
3) Segmented music releases by identifying tracks released as singles, supporting targeted promotion strategies.
4) Evaluated musical characteristics by computing average danceability per album and idenitfying tracks with exceptionally high energy values.
5) Assessed visibility and reach by calculating total views per album and identifying tracks with official videos and strong engagement metrics.

Tools- MS Excel, PostgreSQL

This project demonstrated my proficiency in PostgreSQL to identify actionable insights that support growth, enhance engagement, and inform strategic decision-making.
